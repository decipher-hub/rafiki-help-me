About Rafiki

Rafiki means “friend” in Swahili. We believe dignity and compassion go together — emergency relief when crisis hits, and long-term support so communities can stand on their own.

Compassion first

We listen to communities and design aid that respects culture, safety, and choice.

Transparency

Donors deserve clarity. We track outcomes and share how funds are used.

Sustainability

Beyond aid: training, small business support, and infrastructure that lasts.

Impact you can feel

Schools built, wells drilled, families housed — numbers that represent neighbors, not statistics alone.

Education

Scholarships, supplies, and safe classrooms for children who dream big.

Health & water

Clinics, nutrition, and clean water where illness once spread unchecked.

Livelihoods

Skills, tools, and micro-grants so parents can provide for their families.
