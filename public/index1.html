Our Story

Rafiki means "friend" in Swahili. Founded on the belief that everyone deserves a helping hand, Rafiki Help Me International has been a beacon of hope for communities in need since our inception.

We started with a simple vision: to create a world where no one is left behind. Today, we work across multiple countries, providing essential support to those who need it most — from food and shelter to education and healthcare.

Our approach is rooted in dignity, respect, and sustainable impact. We don't just provide aid; we build partnerships that empower communities to thrive long after our direct involvement ends.

Our Mission

Helping the needy isn't just what we do — it's who we are.

We believe that a friend can need help, and when they do, we must be there. Our mission is to provide immediate relief and long-term sustainable support to communities facing poverty, disaster, and hardship.

Provide emergency food, water, and shelter to those in crisis

Fund education and healthcare for underserved communities

Empower families with skills training and microfinance

Build sustainable infrastructure for lasting change
